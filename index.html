Our Services

APNA SADHAN

APNA SADHAN module helps to provide equipment for harvesting, tilling, sowing, and transportation on rentals basis to the farmers.

APNA KHET

APNA KHET module helps to provide field for agricuture to farmers who need it. It is based on the local ugahi and batai system and to replace them with direct boking and payment.

APNI CHAUPAL

APNI CHAUPAL module is an indigeneous platform for rural talanted youth that will help them to discuss their problems, innovative ideas.

APNI MITTI

APNI MIITI module helps to provide Soil testing facility on demand for farmers.
